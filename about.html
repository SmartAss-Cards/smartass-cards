About Smartass
Support Cards

I lost a lot in a short stretch of time. People, pets, the whole thing. I started SmartAss.Cards (with my wife Vikki) because when life got really dark, the messages that actually helped weren't the tasteful, safe ones. They were the ones that said things like "You're still not funny" or "GFY" (be careful looking that up).

Why did they help? Because that's how certain people who care for one another talk. And it turns out, it's incredibly healing — not just for grief, but for depression, anxiety, pet loss, all of it.

SmartAss.Cards are for people who love someone enough to make them laugh when everything has gone to crap. They're not for everyone. But if you're reading this, they're probably for you.

And definitely for your mom.

You know what I mean.

"Let us put your foot in your mouth for you."
— Shawn Gnandt, Co-Founder Vikki Xiong-Gnandt, Co-Founder
